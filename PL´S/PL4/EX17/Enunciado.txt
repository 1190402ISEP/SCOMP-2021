Special and Normal. While there are free seats, clients enter the movie theater according to their arrival order. 
Whenever the movie theater is full, the entrance of the spectators is conditioned to the exit of other spectators. In 
this case, priority should be given to waiting VIP, then Special, and only then Normal spectators (by that order)
